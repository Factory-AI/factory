Now that your Factory account is set up, you can add users from your organization and assign them the appropriate permission levels as outlined below.

## Outline of Permissions

**Owners:**

* Access Factory

* Configure integrations

* Update roles

* Remove users

**Managers:**

* Access Factory

* Configure integrations

* Update roles

**Users:**

* Access Factory
