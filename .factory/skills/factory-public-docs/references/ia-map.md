## Placement rules

- Put setup and first-run material under Start Here or Platform.
- Put reusable workflows under Guides.
- Put shipped settings, flags, schemas, and CLI options under Reference or Configure & Extend.
- Put admin controls, security, governance, deployment, and analytics under Enterprise.
- Keep hidden reusable snippets under `docs/snippets/`.
- Add redirects for moved URLs in `docs/docs.json`.
